About Clark

Clark Woods self identifies as a “Genderf*cked-Cyborg-Cowboy'' in their artistic practice. Implied by their title, they are a queer non-binary trans artist making works that blend utilize technology as an extension of heir body to blend ‘the real’ and ‘the virtual’, while embodying the spirit of CAMP that has existed in the history of the cowboy. Their works utilize video, 3D/CGI, augmented reality, web-art, and performance to create blended reality experiences of varying degrees and varying forms.
