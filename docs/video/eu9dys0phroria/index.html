EU9dys0PHORIA,
summer 2019

EU9dys0PHORIA (an amalgamation of the words Euphoria and Dysphoria) is an experimental performance-for-video piece about the struggle of navigating the spectrum of gender dysphoria and gender euphoria, and how in Clark, a nonbinary trans person, the same clothes/objects/situations can cause either -phoria on any given day or moment, and the pain of wanting to be a feminie and masculine presenscne in a world with a very rigid idea of femininity and masculinity, among other binaries.
