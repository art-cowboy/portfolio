Clark's Surreal Race,
summer 2021

*best listened to with headphones for the left and right channels*

Clark’s Surreal Race is a sort of parody work made by Clark during the pandemic as an exercise in making works with little resources, produced in their highschool bedroom while in a year long quarantine with their family in Oklahoma, the piece is similar to Rio (2019) in that it is more of an exercise of technique and silliness, and an excuse to perform in front of Clark’s beloved homemade green screen.
